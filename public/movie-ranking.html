My Favorite K-Dramas

Top 3 Thriller/Suspense

Vincenzo

An all-time favorite with a perfect mix of thriller and dark humor. The slow-burn romance adds to the story without taking away from the action.

My Name

A gritty and intense drama with strong action and emotional depth. Still yet to finish it, however, it left a powerful impression.

Strong Woman Do-Bong Soon

A memorable early watch that blends the perfect amount of thriller and suspense with lighthearted moments. Could've done without the romance but it was still so memorable.
